1

Open PeepHub

Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in the bottom-left of the sidebar.

2

Browse & Install

Find a peep you want, click Install. It downloads to ~/.openpeep/peeps/ and is ready immediately. No restart needed.

3

Open a matching file

Click any file that matches the new peep's type. OpenPeep automatically picks the best viewer. Install the Spreadsheet peep, then click a .csv — it opens as a full spreadsheet.
